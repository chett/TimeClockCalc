Time Clock Calculator
See your time spent here!

Input your time in the boxes on the right. If you'd like to add more intervals, click Add Interval.

Your time usage will appear in the upper-right hand corner and will update each time your change your time.

Don't worry about closing your browser window. Your browser will save your intervals (if your browser supports HTML 5).

If you'd like to get rid of your intervals, click Remove All Intervals. This will clear your stored intervals as well.

Feel free to use this tool for whatever you need.
